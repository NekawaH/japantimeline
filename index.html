Japanese Expansion in East Asia, 1931–1945

An interactive historical timeline tracing the trajectory of Imperial Japanese expansionism — from the Mukden Incident to the surrender aboard the USS Missouri — with academic commentary and historiographical reflection.

Select an event from the timeline to view a detailed historical analysis.
Compiled with reference to the historiography of Akira Iriye, John W. Dower, Herbert P. Bix, Iris Chang, Rana Mitter, Eri Hotta, Louise Young, and Mark Peattie.
